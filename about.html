Founded in 2022 in the heart of Harare, Urban Wheels was born from a simple belief: every person deserves access to a vehicle that moves them — not just in miles, but in spirit.

From sleek sedans to rugged SUVs, every vehicle in our collection is hand-picked and rigorously inspected to ensure you drive away with nothing but the best.

At Urban Wheels, we believe the car-buying experience should be as exceptional as the vehicle itself. We've stripped away the pressure, the confusion, and the gimmicks — replacing them with expertise, transparency, and genuine care.

Urban Wheels is a registered company specialising in vehicle imports, clearance and delivery from Japan, Thailand, South Africa, UK and China. We buy vehicles from reputable dealers like Beforward, Autocom, Nikkiyo, Blauda and SBT.
